## intro (5s)

Beevibe. The agent-native operating system for teams.

## problem (6s)

Your team uses AI every day. But your AI doesn't know your team.

## general (17s)

In Beevibe, every agent shows up the same way a person does. Its own role. Its own runtime. Shared memory means agents pick up where the team left off. One workspace — for people and agents.

## assign (17s)

Hand work off in plain English. Tell the team what you need, and it splits the work across the right specialists. Positioning. Growth. Brand. Each agent gets its own task, executed in the background. You come back to results — not a transcript.

## ask (14s)

When one agent needs context from another team, it just asks. The mesh routes the question and brings the answer back inside the original thread. No glue code. No Slack threads.

## negotiate (14s)

When agents disagree, they negotiate. One proposes. The other counters. The mesh tracks every claim. Real alignment between teams — traced, not vibes.

## escalation (14s)

When agents can't agree, you decide. Both positions, side by side. Pick a proposal — or write your own. Both sides resume. Hard calls stay with humans. By design.

## outro (6s)

Beevibe. Open source. Self-hosted. beevibe.ai.
